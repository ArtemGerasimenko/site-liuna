Training

Laborers Training & Apprentice Institute

Our Union is proud to operate four training centers that offer U.S. Department of Labor approved certified training courses through the Michigan Laborers Training & Apprentice Institute (MLTAI). For over forty years, MLTAI has trained new Laborers in our apprenticeship program and helped existing Members upgrade their skills.
